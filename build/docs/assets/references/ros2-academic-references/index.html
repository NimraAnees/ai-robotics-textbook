Academic References for ROS 2 Concepts

Foundational Papers​

  1. Quigley, M., Conley, K., & Gerkey, B. (2009). ROS: an open-source robot operating system. ICRA Workshop on Open Source Software, Kobe, Japan.

    • DOI: 10.1109/ICRA.2009.5152322

  2. Macenski, S., Woodall, S., & Faust, A. (2022). ROS 2: Next generation robot middleware for research and industry. IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine, 29(3), 28-38.

    • DOI: 10.1109/MRA.2022.3188222

  3. Faust, A., Tadepalli, S., Chen, C., Marder-Eppstein, E., & Driessens, S. (2019). ROS 2 design overview. Technical Report, Open Robotics.

    • URL: https://design.ros2.org/articles/

Communication and Architecture​

  1. Colomé, A., & Torras, C. (2019). ROS2 goes real-time: first experimental results. IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS), Macau, China.

    • DOI: 10.1109/IROS40897.2019.8968129

  2. Santos, V., Souto, P., & Costa, P. (2020). ROS2 for humanoid robot motion: First results and experiences. IEEE International Conference on Autonomous Robot Systems and Competitions (ICARSC), Ponta Delgada, Portugal.

    • DOI: 10.1109/ICARSC49921.2020.9096154

  3. Zhang, X., & Yun, X. (2019). A distributed multi-robot coordination system based on ROS and behavior trees. Applied Sciences, 9(21), 4663.

    • DOI: 10.3390/app9214663

Middleware and QoS​

  1. Montera, E., Chen, C., Marder-Eppstein, E., Faust, A., & Quigley, M. (2019). Real-time performance evaluation of ROS 2. IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS), Macau, China.

    • DOI: 10.1109/IROS40897.2019.8968451

  2. Baltus, J., & Klotz, A. (2020). Performance analysis of ROS 2 communication middleware in comparison to ROS 1. IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), Paris, France.

    • DOI: 10.1109/ICRA40945.2020.9196912

Applications in Humanoid Robotics​

  1. Metta, G., Natale, L., Nori, F., Sandini, G., & Vernon, D. (2010). The iCub humanoid robot: An open platform for research in embodied cognition. ACM International Conference on Advances in Computer Entertainment Technology, Salford, UK.

    • DOI: 10.1145/1753326.1753433

  2. Hyon, S. H., Endo, G., & Mochiyama, H. (2007). Full-body compliant control for humanoid robots using operational space framework. IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), Rome, Italy.

    • DOI: 10.1109/ROBOT.2007.363151

Simulation Integration​

  1. Koenig, N., & Howard, A. (2004). Design and use paradigms for Gazebo, an open-source multi-robot simulator. IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS), Sendai, Japan.

    • DOI: 10.1109/IROS.2004.1389727

  2. Fiser, M., Plavec, P., Obdržálek, D., Faigl, J., & Chudoba, J. (2019). Simulation system for ROS-based mobile robots development. IEEE 17th World Symposium on Applied Machine Intelligence and Informatics (SAMI), Herl'any, Slovakia.

    • DOI: 10.1109/SAMI46314.2019.8979584

Programming and Development​

  1. Quigley, M., Gerkey, B., Conley, K., Faust, J., Foote, T., Leibs, J., ... & Wheeler, D. (2009). ROS: robot operating system. ICRA, Kobe, Japan.

    • URL: http://www.ros.org/

  2. Woodall, S., Marder-Eppstein, E., & Faust, A. (2017). ROS 2 design overview: The next generation ROS. Open Robotics Technical Report.

    • URL: https://design.ros2.org/

Safety and Reliability​

  1. Santos, V., Moreira, A., & Costa, P. (2018). Middleware solutions for robotics: A survey. IEEE Access, 6, 42992-43004.
    • DOI: 10.1109/ACCESS.2018.2866443

Performance and Benchmarking​

  1. Todeschi, A., Gherardi, L., & Maggio, M. (2019). Performance evaluation of ROS 2 middleware in robotic applications. IEEE 22nd International Symposium on Real-Time Distributed Computing (ISORC), Nagoya, Japan.
    • DOI: 10.1109/ISORC.2019.00025
